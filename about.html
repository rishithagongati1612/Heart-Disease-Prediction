About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to harness the power of machine learning to improve healthcare and empower individuals with tools to take charge of their heart health. We aim to create accessible, accurate, and easy-to-use applications that help predict the risk of heart disease, allowing users to make informed decisions about their well-being.

Project Overview

The Heart Disease Prediction application is designed to predict the likelihood of heart disease using a variety of health metrics. Built with advanced machine learning algorithms, this project leverages a Random Forest Classifier and a user-friendly Flask-based interface. By inputting details like age, cholesterol levels, and physical activity, users receive a prediction report to help them assess their heart health.

Our system is continually evolving to incorporate new features that enhance accuracy and user experience, such as user login, result downloading, and additional health metrics.
